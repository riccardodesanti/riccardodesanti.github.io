About

I am a guy who needs to hold a vision, or should I say one for each area of life or project on which I work, and tries to see the big picture while staying fully focused on execution details.

Like many others, I realized my passion for technology when I was very young. I’ve learned the basics of web development and tried out the simplest back-end solutions before the age of 14. One year later I started working on a 2 years long startup project within a team of 3 people.
Creating a social network ( based on iOS ) from scratch aiming for perfection, trying to launch it and seeing it failing miserably, I learned many tech, business and leadership skills. Meanwhile I didn’t want to invest energy in my new passion for business I did with leadership. This is why two years ago, while still working on Mobile Development, I took part of a summer Exchange program in Brazil and last year I travelled alone in San Francisco. My desire was to understand better people’s mind in order to lead them towards goals.
Back to Italy I set up a team of six people and led it very seriously in order to found a startup. But eventually I realized my knowledge in both business and tech wasn’t enough to build an interesting tech startup. This is why I decided to put aside for a while the idea of founding a startup and decided to focus on my tech knowledge and skills.
After the first year of University, which I concluded with a GPA of 29,5/30, I've spent the summer doing an Internship as a Software Engineer and trying to explore research areas dealing with Machine Learning.
In the next future I would like to face interesting challenges related with Software Engineering and research in Machine Intelligence.
