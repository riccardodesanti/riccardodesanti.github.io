About

Like many others, I discovered my passion for technology when I was quite young. At high school I’ve developed a simple social network within a team of 3 people and during the first year of University I’ve led a group of 7 people throughout the early stage of a tech startup, eventually realising that I lacked the ability to ideate a deeply innovative product which could actually help the mankind in a meaningful way.
This is the reason why I put aside for a while the idea of founding a company and decided to focus on my knowledge, skills, and general improvement. Doing so, I fell in love with academia, mathematics, research, and could spend a significant amount of time actively nurturing my interests in AI/ML, Math, and Neuroscience. Winning a 2-month long competition, I could join NECSTLab, a research Laboratory of Politecnico di Milano, where I have contributed to a project and eventually presented it at UC Berkeley and Facebook HQ (Menlo Park, CA)
Meanwhile, I’ve completed the BSc with a mark of 110/110 cum laude, and I've joined AIRLab (AI and Robotics Lab), another university lab, where I am conducting theoretical research in Reinforcement Learning supervised by prof. Marcello Restelli.
Next semester I will start an MSc in Machine Intelligence and Theoretical Computer Science at ETH Zürich.
